Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 26, 2025 | Last Updated: August 26, 2025

At HiVaulted, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our post management and video services ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

HiVaulted is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best post management experience:

1.1 Account Information

  • Registration Data: Email address, username, and encrypted password
  • Profile Information: Optional profile details you choose to provide
  • Account Preferences: Settings and customizations you configure

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical Information: Browser type, operating system, device information, and IP address
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our Service, pages visited, and features used
  • Performance Data: Loading times, error rates, and other technical metrics

1.3 Content and Posts

  • Post Content: Videos, images, and text content you upload or create
  • Metadata: File information, timestamps, and processing data
  • Analytics: Performance metrics for your posts and content

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for:

  • Providing and maintaining our post management service
  • Processing and optimizing your video content
  • User authentication and account security
  • Improving user experience and service performance
  • Technical support and customer service
  • Analytics and service optimization
  • Compliance with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication systems
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard cloud security practices
  • Data Backups: Regular backups with encryption
  • Employee Training: Regular security training for all staff

4. Third-Party Services

HiVaulted integrates with selected third-party services:

  • Cloud Storage: For secure content storage and delivery
  • Video Processing: For content optimization and transcoding
  • Analytics: For service improvement and performance monitoring
  • Payment Processing: For billing and subscription management
  • Social Media APIs: For post publishing and management

All third-party services are carefully vetted for security and privacy compliance.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have comprehensive rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent where it's the legal basis

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Account Data: Until account deletion or service termination
  • Content: As long as you maintain your account or as required by law
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated data for up to 2 years
  • Legal Compliance: As required by applicable laws and regulations
  • Security Logs: Up to 1 year for security monitoring

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards through:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by regulatory authorities
  • Adequacy decisions where available
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we discover such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For EU residents, we process data based on:

  • Contract Performance: To provide the requested services
  • Legitimate Interest: For service improvement and security
  • Consent: For optional features and marketing
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Email notification to registered users
  • In-app notifications
  • Prominent notice on our website
  • Updated "Last Modified" date
